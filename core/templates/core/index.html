About Campus ministries

Evangelical Lutheran Church In Kenya Campus Ministry (LCM)

"The Lutheran campus ministry LCM is a recent joint initiative of the Evangelical Lutheran Church in Kenya and Swedish Lutheran Evangelical association in Finland. This ministry endeavours to reach out especially to the ELCK students in their higher learning institution's environments i.e colleges and universities. The need and thought for such a ministry has existed for a long time due to the enormous challenges that the students go through when they leave their home environments. The focus of the ministry is Gospel centred there by bringing Christ to the people and people to Christ through the means of grace.
